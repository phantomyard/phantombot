# Phantombot Growth Campaign Ideas

## 1. The "De-enshitification" Twitter Thread
- **Tweet 1:** AI agents are getting bloated. We're spending more time fighting abstractions than getting work done. It's time for a "Spartan" approach to agency. Introducing Phantombot. 🧵
- **Tweet 2:** The core problem: Most agent platforms try to "translate" tool calls. This adds latency, fragility, and friction. Our insight: The harness (Claude, Pi, Gemini) can do its own tools. Let it.
- **Tweet 3:** Phantombot is a 98MB single binary. No Node. No Python. No Docker. Just a high-torque bridge between your persona and the best CLI harnesses in the world.
- **Tweet 4:** It gives your agent a Soul (Identity + Memory + Telegram). Voice-in, voice-out. Scheduled tasks. Local SQLite persistence. Privacy-first, performance-obsessed.
- **Tweet 5:** We built it because we were tired of "enshitified" gateways that fail to restart or bloat your host. Phantombot updates in <2s and stays out of the model's way.
- **Tweet 6:** Ready to give your assistant a Soul? Check out the repo: https://github.com/phantomyard/phantombot 🚀
